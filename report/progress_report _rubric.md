The motivations and goals of the project are presented concisely, and the rationale for using deep learning is clearly explained with sufficient and professional supporting elements.
	
5 to >3.5 pts Full Marks The project description is clear, concise, and compelling. The motivation and goals are well defined, showing strong understanding of the problem context. The reason for applying deep learning is logically justified with professional support (e.g., references, domain reasoning, or examples). Presentation is coherent and polished.
3.5 to >2.0 pts Meets Expectation The project’s motivation and goals are stated but somewhat limited in depth or clarity. The rationale for using deep learning is mentioned but not fully developed or supported. Writing is generally clear but lacks strong professional framing or context.
2 to >0 pts Needs improvement The motivation, goals, or rationale for deep learning are vague or incomplete. The explanation may be confusing, lack focus, or fail to demonstrate an understanding of why deep learning is relevant to the project.
